Why invest in bare ownership

An investment product that doesn't exist in the traditional market

01
Entry price well below market
You acquire ownership of a property in a high-demand area at a fraction of its real value, with appreciation potential until full ownership consolidates.
02
No mortgage or bank financing needed
This is a direct cash investment. You don't depend on interest rates, credit approvals or changing financial market conditions.
03
You can resell before full consolidation
You're not tied in indefinitely. Bare ownership is transferable to third parties at any time, respecting the original owner's usufruct rights.
04
Full legal security
Every transaction is notarized and registered in the Public Property Registry. You receive deeds that certify your bare ownership title.
05
Due diligence included
Before presenting any property, we verify registry history, legal status and formal valuation. You decide with complete information.
06
Virtually no competition in Mexico
Unlike Spain or France where bare ownership is a mature market, in Mexico it's an emerging niche. Entering today means entering before the competition arrives.
